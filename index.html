About Me

A Public Health Specialist currently working at Karesi Provincial Health District. He graduated from medical school in 2019. After a brief experience in the Emergency Department, residency training at Dokuz Eylül University commenced the same year under the supervision of Prof. Alp Ergör. The residency thesis was focused on Transmission-Related Factors and Prediction of Infection Probability in Healthcare Workers Exposed to SARS-CoV-2 .

Research has been conducted in occupational health for healthcare workers, COVID-19 epidemiology, surveillance, and geographic information systems. In 2022, the role of Coordinator of the Research Assistants Working Group under the Turkish Society of Public Health Specialists was undertaken.

Editorial responsibilities have also been assumed for the Sosyal Bilimler ve Sağlık Bülteni (SoSa) . A keen interest in technology and a history as a dedicated gamer combine with professional endeavors to embrace innovation in public health.
